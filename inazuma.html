Inazuma City

Inazuma City is a subarea and a main city located in Narukami Island, Inazuma. Rising high atop the cliffs at the southern tip of Narukami Island, it it was built during the Archon War and is the seat of power of the Shogunate and the Raiden Shogun.

Watatsumi

Watatsumi Island is an area located in Inazuma. Among these islands, it is located the farthest and on the opposite end of Narukami Island. It is known for its dense foliage in breathtaking colors which are distinct from plants found elsewhere.

Mosoujin Gorge

Musoujin Gorge is located in the eastern part of Yashiori Island, Inazuma. It was formed during a battle between the Raiden Shogun and Orobashi. The Raiden Shogun performed Musou no Hitotachi, her divine judgement, and cleaved both the serpent and the island.

Amakumo Peak

Amakumo Peak is a subarea located in Seirai Island, Inazuma. Long ago, Kanna Kapatcir was slain here (thunder manifestation), while the power of its lingering hatred and regret was suppressed by wards maintained by the Asase Shrine.

Chinju Forest

Chinju Forest is a subarea located in Narukami Island, Inazuma. It is a sacred grove nestled in a small river valley just south of Mt. Yougou. The path to the Kamisato Estate leads through these woods. Due to some unknown reason, this forest is shrouded in an eternal night, with the glow of an unseen moon casting a serene ambience within its borders.

Asase Shrine

The Asase Shrine is a subarea in Seirai Island. The shrine was established to suppress the lingering power of Kanna Kapatcir, also known as the Thunderbird.







CHARACTER SHOWCASE

Raiden Shogun

The Raiden Shogun is comprised of two beings in one body: Ei, the current Electro Archon of Inazuma; and the Shogun, the puppet created by Ei to act as the ruler of Inazuma in her stead.

Arataki Itto

Despite appearing as "mean and overbearing," Itto is a kind soul at heart, apart from trying to pick a fight with Kujou Sara at every possible opportunity. He is not particularly intelligent, but can put in the effort when asked of him. He is respected by his gang and mostly liked by Inazuma City's people, especially children, but is at odds with the Tenryou Commission despite his mostly harmless antics and pranks.

Yoimiya

A talented pyrotechnician. The current owner of Naganohara Fireworks known as the "Queen of the Summer Festival". A girl filled with fiery passion. The uncompromising childish innocence and the obsession with craftsmanship intertwine in her to create a spectacular blaze.

Sangonomiya Kokomi

Kokomi is the Divine Priestess of Watatsumi Island, and also serves as its supreme leader. She is well-versed in the art of war, is good at strategizing, and has keen insights into military affairs. She is also adept at handling domestic affairs, diplomacy, and other matters. Still, this unfathomable leader has a mysterious side to her...

Kamisato Ayaka

Daughter of the Yashiro Commission's Kamisato Clan from Inazuma. Dignified and elegant, wise and determined. Sincere and pleasant to others. Universally loved by the Inazuma people, she has earned the title of Shirasagi Himegimi.
