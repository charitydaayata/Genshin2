Children of Echoes

The Children of Echoes, or Nanatzcayan, is one of the six tribes in Natlan and is home of the saurian Tepetlisaur. They can be found at the "Children of Echoes" inside the gorge in Tequemecan Valley. Their current chief is Pacal.

Scions of Canopy Saurian

The Scions of the Canopy, or Huitztlan, is one of the six tribes in Natlan. They can be found at the "Scions of the Canopy" suspended off the cliff-face of the Coatepec Mountain.

People of the Springs Saurian

It is said that the man who would become the first chief of the People of the Springs went on an adventure in search of the legendary mysterious island. With the help and trust of his band of companions, he reached the destination. However, when faced with the final peril, he chose to abandon the "outcome" to save his companions.

Flower Feather Clan

Members of this tribe bond well to the Qucusaurus, and many able-bodied tribespeople join the Qucusaur Riders, as an active or reserve member. Regardless of position, they must pass a very tough entrance exam, which involves being a proficient warrior and being able to ride a qucusaur, requiring the applicant to be able to gain their trust.

Masters of the Nightwind

Out of all of the tribes, the Masters of the Night-Wind have the deepest connection with the Night Kingdom. Their primary role is as "shamans" (or "mystics") who conduct rituals to communicate with the Wayob for knowledge and guidance. They are also responsible for monitoring the health of Natlan's Ley Lines and Phlogiston.

Stadium of the Sacred Flames

Stadium of the Sacred Flame is a subarea and a main city located in Basin of Unnumbered Flames, Natlan. It is considered the core landmark of Natlan, and houses a continually burning fire called the Sacred Flame. The stadium is the site of the Pilgrimage of the Return of the Sacred Flame, a tournament in which members of the six tribes compete to select the nation's strongest warriors.







CHARACTER SHOWCASE

Xilonen

Though Xilonen has a laid-back personality and emphasizes the importance of leisure time, she also takes her work very seriously, researching books and ancient texts to the point that her workshop is overcrowded with them. Xilonen focuses on the technical aspects of things rather than abstract concepts, and during her education, only began to be intrigued in the storytelling aspect of Ancient Name forging by the thought exercise of cross-referencing stories to form something tangible.

KInich

To the general public, the Saurian Hunter appears pragmatic, cold, and to-the-point. Steady, direct, and efficient, he has a utilitarian approach to life, and his exterior coldness tends to discourage people around him from getting close. Appearing to understand more than he lets on, Kinich prefers to keep a distance from others and let everyone else do the talking.

Mavuika

When the name "Mavuika" is mentioned, the citizens of Natlan will not be stingy with their praise, for she has proven herself well-qualified and worthy of respect, be it as a leader or Archon. She is the pinnacle of strength in Natlan, and she has the bearing to match. Even newly-arrived travelers need only hear her speak at the Stadium of the Sacred Flame to know that she merits being known as the sun.

Mualani

Mualani is a cheerful young woman who enjoys making friends and bringing out the best from others, like many other people from her tribe. She is much more enthusiastic and enjoys watersports, especially surfing. She is quick to befriend new people, stating that every person that one hasn't met is simply a friend that hadn't been met yet. This is particularly true with the Traveler and Paimon, where she quickly befriends the two upon meeting them and invites them to her tribe to enjoy their hospitality.

Chasca

Chasca hails from the Flower-Feather Clan. She is afflicted with a rare disease that caused her biological parents to abandon her in the wilds, though she survived and ended up being adopted by a group of Qucusaurs. Eventually, upon returning to human society, she was adopted by Chuychu's parents. Among the tribes in Natlan, she is regarded as a peacekeeper, resolving tribal disputes without difficulty.
